What Is The Purpose Of This?

Beyond All Reason is a very comprehensive RTS game. Unfortunately for a rare and hidden gem like BAR, there is no dedicated wiki or database for the game.

Initially, the whole purpose of the site is to serve as a sort of forum for players to make guides for the game. But we the developers of the site wish to eventually make this into a full full fledged unofficial wiki of sorts.

We are in no way shape or form related to the passionate and talented dev team of Beyond All Reason. But given that the game is an open-source project by many passionate RTS players, we'd say we are very much so contributing to the passionate community of Beyond All Reason!
